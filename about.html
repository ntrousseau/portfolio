About Noelle Rousseau

I graduated from SCAD in Savannah, Georgia with a BFA in Illustration and I'm currently studying Web Development at Wake Tech Community College.

My artwork pushes the boundaries between Fine Art and Illustration. It's full of vibrant colors and high contrast. Materials are always the most important aspect of each of my pieces because I LOVE textures! If I can get away with it, I try to use as many materials as possible. Color choices are intentional because they have the power to alter mood and emotion.

After college, I traveled all over South America; Ecuador, Peru, Bolivia, Chile, Argentina, and Uruguay. I ended up living on the Ecuadorian coast for a year generating income from commissioned murals and paintings, waiting tables, bartending... My journeys over the years have definitely influenced my artwork style.

I have a background in Graphic Design and Content Management and I currently serve as a Graphics Specialist for the Sister Study, a longitudinal cohort study that studies the role of gene-environment interactions in breast cancer risk where I fulfill graphic design and website development needs.

In my spare time, I create illustrations and freelance graphic design. I specialize in children's books and editorial illustration. I also design websites, logos, posters, flyers, and all things related to graphic design.
